Logo Treatments


Objective

The objective of my design was to create a logo that looked modern but had some originality at the same time. I wanted to have a logo that brought out my initials but tied into a concept that conveyed creativity and storytelling. A lightbulb was used as this metaphor since it is used to symbolize a creative idea in popular culture. The filaments inside a traditional bulb gave me an idea of how to integrate my initials so that the design was as tight as possible.
